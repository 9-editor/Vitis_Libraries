Detailed Documentation
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Mersenne Twister to generate uniform random number. Although its period is shorter than MT19937 but also long enough in most cases. It also offers flexcilibily in parrelel computing which may demand indepenency in multiple instance of random number generators.
