rho the correlation of the 2 Weiner processes
T the expiration time
r the risk free interest rate
kappa the rate of reversion
vvol the volatility of volatility (sigma)
vbar the long term average variance (theta)
w_max the w value to integrate up to
dw the delta w for the integration
padding the pragma data pack requires structure size to be a power of 2
