template struct xf::fintech::kernel_parameters
==============================================

.. toctree::
	:hidden:

.. code-block:: cpp
	:class: overview-code-block

	#include <hcf_engine.hpp>




.. rubric:: Parameters:

.. list-table::
    :widths: 20 80

    *
        - DT

        - supported data type including double and float data type, which decides the precision of result, default is float.

    *
        - s0

        - the stock price at t=0

    *
        - v0

        - the stock price variance at t=0

    *
        - K

        - the strike price

    *
        - rho

        - the correlation of the 2 Weiner processes

    *
        - T

        - the expiration time

    *
        - r

        - the risk free interest rate

    *
        - kappa

        - the rate of reversion

    *
        - vvol

        - the volatility of volatility (sigma)

    *
        - vbar

        - the long term average variance (theta)

    *
        - w_max

        - the w value to integrate up to

    *
        - dw

        - the delta w for the integration

    *
        - padding

        - the pragma data pack requires structure size to be a power of 2
