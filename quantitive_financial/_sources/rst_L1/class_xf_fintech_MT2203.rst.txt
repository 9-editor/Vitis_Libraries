Overview
~~~~~~~~

Mersenne Twister to generate uniform random number. Although its period is shorter than :ref:`MT19937 <doxid-classxf_1_1fintech_1_1_m_t19937>` but also long enough in most cases. It also offers flexcilibily in parrelel computing which may demand indepenency in multiple instance of random number generators. :ref:`More...<details-doxid-classxf_1_1fintech_1_1_m_t2203>`
